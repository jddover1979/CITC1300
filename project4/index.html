About Us

Happy Pet was founded when we saw a need in one local neighboorhood to help abandonded pets find new homes. After years of planning and searching for a headquarters, we found an old drug store building that was retrofitted with the appropriate facilities to shelter, feed and bathe new potential pets to prepare them for their new homes!

Mission Statment

To place deserving pets with much needed and welcoming homes.
