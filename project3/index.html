Final Fantasy XIV Online Healer Jobs

Seeing as how I couldn't find another topic that would fit around the idea of flexboxes, I'll risk revealing my inner nerd by using my CSS flexbox project to describe the healer jobs in FFXIV since they use these nifty icons.

White Mage

A healing powerhouse -- slow to cast but very powerful. Limited mitigation but array of techniques more than makes up for this loss.

Scholar

Mitigative master -- prevents damage from occuring in the first place, limiting the need for reactionary healing.

Astrologian

Jack-of-all-trades -- decent mitigation and faster casts, but healing output is lower. Has the ability to offer damage buffs that the other jobs don't allow.
